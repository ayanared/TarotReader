Instructions

Tarot is a divination tool used to gain clarity about any situation. For this particular spread, the first card represents the past. Use this card to gain more clarity on the past influences that affect your query. The second card represents the current influences that affect your query. It answers the question: what is going on for you right now? The last card represents the future. It answers the questions: Where am I going with this? What am going to get out of it?
